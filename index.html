ABOUT

Nathan P. Keene is a Web designer and content developer with deep prior background in education, copywriting and content development, especially interested in integrating these skills in a unified production context.

He is comfortable coding with essential front end Web technologies: JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, JSON, CSS3 and HTML5.

And he's a darn quick study, too! He continues to add skills all the time and sees no reason to stop.

Meanwhile, he is especially interested in opportunities to pair his coding expertise with previous background in education, test design and content development to create compelling Web experiences that target user learning or information.

Nathan P. Keene has recently completed Code 301 at Code Fellows.

He lives in Portland, Oregon with his wife, Astrid, their delightful twin daughters Julius and Nikko, and their totem animal, Merlin. He recently saw an absolutely marvelous canoe he wanted. Nathan P. Keene fervently hopes to build a Little Cabin in Alaska, as that is the land of his provenance, and additionally one day to Save the Earth from a Giant Meteorite.
