ABOUT

You might call Nathan P. Keene an alien but he prefers the term Ausländer. He has dealt in a number of trades and especially in teaching. He is interested in testing and evaluation, but more deeply in (as they call users in education) student-centered experience.

"But WAIT! What does this have to do with us?" you might readily now ask if you happen to be a hiring manager in a tech-intensive profession and Nathan P. Keene has asked your consideration of his recruiting materials, or yet again, if you are someone in some other trade who happens to be looking for the services of a freelance (Nathan P. Keene prefers the term independent) Web developer versed in the ways of JavaScript, jQuery, JSON, AJAX, GitHub, CSS3 and HTML5 and eager to make his first mark in the etheric world.

The answer is simply that Nathan P. Keene possesses not only the skills, qualities and expertise of the first domain mentioned above, but he now has acquired those of the second, which to many he expects will make him an ideal candidate for the positions they have in mind.

Nathan P. Keene has recently completed Code 301 at Code Fellows.

He lives in Portland, Oregon with his wife, Astrid, his delightful twin daughters Julius and Nikko, and their totem animal, Merlin. He recently saw an absolutely marvelous canoe he wanted. Nathan P. Keene fervently hopes to build a Little Cabin in Alaska, as that is the land of his provenance, and additionally one day to Save the Earth from a Giant Meteorite.
